    Information about public transportation in new york

    Project maintained by m2rtin Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    This is an automated Public Transport Information service for New York. It allows people to use natural language which does not require users look and touch the screens of their phone, which can be really useful for blind people.Try now!

    Capabilities:

    • route information around New York city, New Jersey transit utilizing MTA datasets
    • railroad information across large US cities
    • weather forecast for cities across US
    • time information for cities across US (time zones)
