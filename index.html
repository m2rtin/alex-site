    Information about public transportation in New York

    This is an automated Public Transport Information service for New York. Alex is the easiest way to get around town for the visually-impaired. This project is competing in MTA App Quest 3.0 in a Accessibility Innovation category. You are more than welcome to explore and try our service out. Please don't forget to vote for us!

    This work is developed as a part of my Master Thesis project under supervision of Dr. Filip Jurcicek.
