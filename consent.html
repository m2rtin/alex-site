    • TERMS OF USE

    All interactions with the dialogue system are recorded for further study and commercial deployment of spoken dialogue systems and similar applications involving spoken speech.

    For the purposes of the Czech Republic 101/2000 Data Protection Act, you give your permission for audio and video recordings, transcriptions, and non-identifying information (e.g. gender, age, mother tongue) to be electronically stored, utilized and shared for purposes of research, development, and commercial deployment of spoken dialogue systems and similar applications involving spoken speech. No identifying information, gathered in this study will be disclosed to any persons other than the researchers and their collaborators.

    Results of work with collected data will appear in academic journals in terms of group data. If any individual data will be presented, the data will be totally anonymous, without any means of identifying the individuals involved. The researchers will be glad to answer any questions you might have. However, answers that could influence the outcome of the work will be deferred until the work is completed.

    Every effort is made to conduct this work in compliance with the appropriate professional guidelines.
