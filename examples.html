    • EXAMPLE DIALOGUES

    Alex introduces an easy way to get to subway, bus, rail, ferry and monorail transit information around NYC and NJ. You can specify your location and destination and it will provide you with the first connection. You may further specify your trip requirements such as relative or absolute departure and arrival times or preferred vehicle. You can cycle through few options back and forth in a given timespan. You can subsequently ask for additional information such as relative or absolute departure and arrival time, names of origin, destination, transfer count, current time and duration. Furthermore, You can ask about the weather in any US city and the time zone in any state.
